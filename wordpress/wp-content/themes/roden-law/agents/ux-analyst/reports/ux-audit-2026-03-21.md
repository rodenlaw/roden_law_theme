# Roden Law UX Audit & Competitor Benchmark Report

**Date:** March 21, 2026
**Audited:** rodenlaw.com (desktop + mobile at 390px)
**Competitors Reviewed:** Spiva Law Group, Steinberg Law Firm, Joye Law Firm, The Eichholz Law Firm, Yarborough Applegate
**Markets:** Savannah GA, Charleston SC, Columbia SC, Myrtle Beach SC

---

## Executive Summary

Roden Law's site is stronger than most competitors on desktop — the above-the-fold form, trust stats, and phone CTA are well-placed. However, the site has critical mobile conversion gaps: no visible phone number in the sticky header on mobile, form fields that don't stack properly on small screens, and a live chat widget that overlaps the contact form. Competitors like Steinberg and Joye also have Spanish language support and accessibility widgets that Roden Law lacks entirely.

---

## P0 — Critical (Fix Immediately, Highest Revenue Impact)

### Homepage/Sitewide: No Phone Number Visible on Mobile Header
**Priority:** P0
**Impact:** Conversion
**Current State:** On mobile (<768px), the top bar with phone number is hidden, and the header phone CTA disappears. The only phone number visible is below the fold after scrolling past the hero text, stats bar, and guarantee line. An injured person on their phone has to scroll 2+ screens to find how to call.
**Recommendation:** Add a persistent click-to-call phone icon or button in the sticky mobile header, next to the hamburger menu. This should be a `tel:` link with the local or vanity number, always visible.
**Competitor Reference:** Steinberg keeps their (843) 720-2800 visible in header at all breakpoints. Joye has a large orange phone block that persists on scroll. Yarborough Applegate keeps 843-972-0150 in the header bar on mobile. Every major competitor keeps phone numbers visible on mobile.
**Implementation Notes:** Add a phone icon button to `header.php` that's `display: none` on desktop and `display: flex` on mobile. Position it to the left of the hamburger menu.

---

### Sitewide: Live Chat Widget Overlaps Contact Form Sidebar
**Priority:** P0
**Impact:** Conversion
**Current State:** The live chat bubble (bottom-right) directly overlaps the "Free Case Review" sidebar form on practice area pages and the homepage form on mobile. On the car accident lawyers pillar page, the chat widget covers the form's "Last Name" field and part of the phone field.
**Recommendation:** Reposition the live chat widget to bottom-left on pages with sidebar forms, or add CSS to push the form sidebar up with sufficient bottom margin. Alternatively, collapse the chat to a smaller icon that doesn't overlap form fields.
**Competitor Reference:** Steinberg places their live chat as a left-side "LIVE CHAT" tab (vertical text), which avoids form overlap entirely.
**Implementation Notes:** This likely requires configuration in the chat widget's dashboard (position setting) or a CSS override.

---

### Mobile: Contact Form Name Fields Don't Stack
**Priority:** P0
**Impact:** Conversion / Mobile
**Current State:** The "First Name" and "Last Name" fields remain side-by-side on mobile (390px viewport), resulting in ~150px-wide input fields. These are cramped and difficult to type into on a phone. The `.rsf-row.rsf-half` grid uses `grid-template-columns: 1fr 1fr` with no mobile breakpoint to collapse.
**Recommendation:** Add a media query at `max-width: 600px` to stack name fields vertically: `grid-template-columns: 1fr`.
**Competitor Reference:** Industry standard for PI law forms is single-column on mobile. Steinberg and Joye both use full-width single fields on mobile.
**Implementation Notes:** CSS fix: `@media (max-width: 600px) { .rsf-row.rsf-half { grid-template-columns: 1fr; } }`

## P1 — High Priority (Significant Conversion Impact)

### Sitewide: No Spanish Language Support
**Priority:** P1
**Impact:** Conversion / Trust / Accessibility
**Current State:** No Spanish-language option. The GA/SC markets have significant Spanish-speaking populations.
**Recommendation:** Add a "Se Habla Espanol" link in the header, leading to at minimum a Spanish-language contact page with a translated form and key firm stats.
**Competitor Reference:** 4 of 4 competitors reviewed offer Spanish language support: Steinberg, Joye, Eichholz, Yarborough. This is table stakes for PI firms in these markets.
**Implementation Notes:** Start with a single `/espanol/` landing page. Full site translation can follow.

---

### Sitewide: No Accessibility Widget
**Priority:** P1
**Impact:** Accessibility / Trust / Legal Risk
**Current State:** No accessibility overlay, no visible accessibility statement, no WCAG compliance indicator. For a firm serving injured people — many of whom may have vision impairment, TBI, or mobility issues — this is a significant gap.
**Recommendation:** Add an accessibility widget (e.g., accessiBe, UserWay) and an accessibility statement page.
**Competitor Reference:** Steinberg, Joye, and Eichholz all have accessibility widgets.
**Implementation Notes:** Can be added via a single JS snippet in `header.php` or `functions.php`.

---

### Homepage: Hero Section Lacks Emotional Urgency
**Priority:** P1
**Impact:** Conversion / Trust
**Current State:** The H1 is descriptive but generic. No imagery of real people, no emotional hook. The hero background is a solid navy color.
**Recommendation:** Add a background image of the attorneys (professional team photo) or a brief video. Add a short empathy-driven subhead.
**Competitor Reference:** Spiva uses a full-bleed team photo with video. Steinberg uses their full team walking on a Charleston cobblestone street. Both communicate "real people, real firm."
**Implementation Notes:** A professional team photo of the 8 attorneys would significantly elevate the hero.

---

### Practice Area Pages: No Inline CTA Between Content Sections
**Priority:** P1
**Impact:** Conversion
**Current State:** The pillar pages have long-form content but the only CTAs are in the sticky sidebar form and the phone button below the hero. A user reading mid-page content has to scroll back up or to the bottom.
**Recommendation:** Insert an inline CTA banner between major content sections (after every 2-3 sections).
**Competitor Reference:** Steinberg intersperses case result callouts and CTA buttons throughout their practice area content.
**Implementation Notes:** The theme already has `templates/parts/inline-cta-banner.php` — verify it's being called in practice area templates.

---

### Sitewide: Form Lacks Validation Feedback and Success State
**Priority:** P1
**Impact:** Conversion
**Current State:** No visual required field indicators, no inline validation, no success message after submission, no loading state on submit button.
**Recommendation:** (1) Add asterisks to required fields. (2) Highlight invalid fields on blur. (3) Show success message after submission. (4) Add loading state to submit button.
**Implementation Notes:** Changes in `js/main.js` and `inc/template-tags.php`. Success message should emphasize speed: "We'll call you within 15 minutes."

## P2 — Medium Priority (Meaningful UX Improvement)

### Homepage: Case Results Carousel Partially Hidden by Chat Widget
**Priority:** P2
**Impact:** Trust / Conversion
**Current State:** The 4th case result card is partially cut off by the live chat widget.
**Recommendation:** Add right padding to the carousel container or limit visible items to 3 with clear navigation arrows.
**Competitor Reference:** Steinberg shows case results in a full-width bar — no overlap.
**Implementation Notes:** CSS fix: add `padding-right: 80px` to carousel container.

---

### Mobile: Top Bar Trust Messaging Disappears
**Priority:** P2
**Impact:** Trust
**Current State:** "Serving Georgia & South Carolina — No Fees Unless We Win" and "Free 24/7 Consultations" are hidden on mobile.
**Recommendation:** Condense to a single line on mobile or move trust signals into the mobile hero section.
**Competitor Reference:** Spiva keeps "CALL FOR A FREE CONSULTATION 24/7" visible at all breakpoints.
**Implementation Notes:** Modify `header.php` media queries to show a condensed version.

---

### Intersection Pages: No Embedded Map
**Priority:** P2
**Impact:** Trust / Local SEO
**Current State:** "Get Directions" button exists but no embedded Google Map on the page.
**Recommendation:** Add a small embedded Google Map below the NAP block on intersection pages.
**Implementation Notes:** The `_roden_map_embed` meta field exists but may not be rendered on intersection pages.

---

### Practice Area Pages: Location Matrix Shows URL Slugs
**Priority:** P2
**Impact:** Trust / UX
**Current State:** The office cards show raw URL slugs (e.g., `/car-accident-lawyers/savannah-ga/`) instead of addresses or descriptions.
**Recommendation:** Replace with office address or service area description.
**Implementation Notes:** Modify `templates/parts/location-matrix.php` to pull from `roden_firm_data()`.

---

### Homepage: Attorney Photos Are Small
**Priority:** P2
**Impact:** Trust
**Current State:** 4 attorneys shown in ~120px circular headshots. Hard to see.
**Recommendation:** Increase to 180-200px. Add titles/roles beneath names.
**Implementation Notes:** CSS change to `.attorney-card img` dimensions.

### Homepage: No Video Content
